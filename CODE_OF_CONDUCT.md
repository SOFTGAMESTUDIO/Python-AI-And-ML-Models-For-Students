# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

> **Governed by Soft Game Studio**

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming educational environment for students and enthusiasts alike, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:
* Using welcoming and inclusive language.
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences, especially recognizing that many users are students learning AI/ML for the first time.
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
* Focusing on what is best for the educational community.
* Showing empathy towards other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:
* The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
* Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
* Public or private harassment.
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission.
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or educational setting.

## Commercial Prohibition Enforcement

As explicitly stated by **Soft Game Studio**, the distribution or sale of this codebase—or localized derivatives acting as standalone or packed commercial products—whether online or offline, is **strictly prohibited**. 

Violation of this commercial prohibition rule is considered a severe breach of this Code of Conduct and will result in permanent banning from the community and potential legal action to protect our educational copyright. 

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior (including attempting to monetize the repository) may be reported by contacting the Soft Game Studio maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
